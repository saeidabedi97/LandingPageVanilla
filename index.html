Why NoGa?

Did you know that 33% of all food produced is wasted every year? if 25% of the currently wasted food was saved, it could feed up to 870 millions hungry people.

Fresko is here to reduce wasted food because reducing food waste can generate a triple win:

  1. For the economy
  2. For food security
  3. For the environment

Features

Works offline

Access the application even if there is no signals. Search for any product mode in offline mode.

Vegetarian mode

Are you a vegetarian and afraid to eat non-vegetarian food? Just enable the vegetarian mode and get notified when the product contains non-vegetarian ingredients.

Recipes

Want a recipe of the product you scanned? Sure, Fresko provides you detailed recipes.

How the app works?

/1

Sign up / login

The first you need to do is to create an account in Fresko, you can sign up with an email. You can login using the email you signed up with or sign in with Google.

/2

products to your list

You can then start adding products to your list by simply taking pictures of the code of the product and the product will be added to the list with name, expiration date and how many days are left until it expires. The order of the products will be based on how soon the it will expire.

/3

Notifications

The app will send you notifications whenever a product is about to expire in the next couple of days.
